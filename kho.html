KHO KHO


Originate: Kho Kho, originating from ancient India, is a traditional tag game.

Player: game played by two teams of 12 players each, with 9 players on the field at a time.

Ground: The game is conducted on a rectangular field measuring 27 by 16 meters, divided into two halves with central lanes.

The chasing team attempts to tag opponents who try to evade them while running between the lanes. Kho Kho emphasizes speed, agility, and quick reflexes, and it remains a popular sport in Indian schools and communities.
