CRICKET


Originate: Cricket originated in England in the 16th century and has evolved into a globally popular sport.

Player: 11 players

Ground: oval-shaped field with a 22-yard-long pitch at the center.

The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding, with teams taking turns to score runs. Cricket grounds vary in size but typically have a diameter ranging from 450 to 500 feet. Iconic formats of the game include Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) games.
