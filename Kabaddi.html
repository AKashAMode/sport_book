KABADDI


Originate: Kabaddi has ancient roots in South Asia, with references dating back over 4,000 years.

Player: 7 players

Ground: seven players each on a rectangular court measuring 10 by 13 meters.

The objective is for a single raider from one team to tag as many defenders as possible and return to their half without being tackled. Kabaddi combines physical prowess and tactical acumen, and the sport has gained international recognition through leagues and competitions like the Pro Kabaddi League.
