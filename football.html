FOOTBALL


Originate: Football, or soccer, traces its origins back to ancient civilizations but took its modern form in England in the 19th century.

Player: 11 players

Ground: rectangular field measuring 100-110 meters in length and 64-75 meters in width.

The game involves advancing a ball into the opponent's goal without using hands, with the team scoring the most goals winning. Football is celebrated worldwide for its simplicity and thrilling matches, highlighted in tournaments like the FIFA World Cup.
