VOLLEYBALL


Originate:Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan in the United States.

Player: 6 players

Ground: Each on a court divided by a net, measuring 18 meters long and 9 meters wide.

The objective is to send the ball over the net and ground it on the opponent's side while preventing the same on their side. Matches are typically played in sets, with teams needing to win a majority of sets to claim victory. Volleyball is renowned for its dynamic play and teamwork, popular both indoors and on beaches.
